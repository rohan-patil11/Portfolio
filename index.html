Professional Summary

Highly decorated and results-driven retired Brigadier from the Indian Army's elite Corps of Army Air Defence, offering over three decades of distinguished service in defense strategy, air defense operations, cross-functional leadership, and high-level military training.

Adept at leading large, multidisciplinary teams under high-stakes conditions, formulating and executing strategic defense policies, and advising senior government and military leadership. Proven record of successfully transitioning operational expertise into training, logistics, administration, and defense consulting roles, with deep domain knowledge in military modernization, crisis response, and international defense cooperation.

Currently serving as Additional Director General, Directorate of Resettlement Zone (West), facilitating the transition of retiring armed forces personnel into second careers.
